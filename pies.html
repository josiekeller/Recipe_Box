Banana Cream Pie

View Recipe >>

Ingredients

  • 2 T butter
  • 1/4 C corn starch
  • 3/4 C sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 C milk
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 large bananas
  • baked pie crust
  • whipping cream

Instructions

  1. Melt butter
  2. Add corn starch, sugar, and salt
  3. Slowly add milk
  4. Stir constantly until mixture thickens
  5. Add beaten egg yolks
  6. Cook 4 min
  7. Add vanilla
  8. Cool and add to baked pie crust
  9. Slice bananas on top of custard
  10. Top with whipped cream

Apricot Horns

View Recipe >>

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Crisco
  • 2 yeast packets
  • 14 oz evaporated milk
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 7 C flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 lb dried apricots

Apricot Filling

  1. Simmer dried apricots and water (to cover) until soft
  2. Blend boiled apricots and water until smooth

Cookie Dough

  1. Heat evaporated milk
  2. Combine salt, yeast, & flour
  3. Blend in Crisco
  4. Add hot milk and beaten eggs
  5. Form 14-15 baseball-sized balls of dough

Cookies

  1. Roll dough out in raw sugar
  2. Cut into 16 pie-shaped pieces
  3. Put a small dollop of apricot fillin on the thick end of each triangle
  4. Roll into croissant shapes
  5. Bake 10-12 min @ 400°

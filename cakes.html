Sharon's Cupcakes

View Recipe >>

Ingredients

  • 2 C sugar
  • 1/2 C vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 6 T cocoa
  • 2 1/2 C flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 C buttermilk
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2/3 C Crisco
  • 1/2 C granulated sugar
  • 1/3 C evaporated milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Cupcakes

  1. Cream together sugar, oil, vanilla, & egg
  2. Sift together cocoa, flour, salt, buttermilk, & soda
  3. Bake 20 min @ 350°
  4. Makes 28

Filling

  1. Beat together Crisco, sugar, evaporated milk, salt, & vanilla to form cream
  2. After cupcakes are cool, cut center out and fill with filling
